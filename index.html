What We Do

Supplementing your team's ability to successfully implement new technology and improve the customer experience at your financial institution.

Core Banking Conversions

"Core system conversions are like performing open heart surgery on a patient that's awake," said one financial institution CEO. Fortunately, they're a rare event — but that also means your team has little experience with them. Coppermoon can help manage project plans, warn about troubling patterns, and assist workstream leaders in testing and coordination.

Digital Banking Implementation

Online banking, mobile banking, bill pay, P2P payments, remote deposit capture, lending systems, payment processing, and card management platform rollouts for banks and credit unions. From vendor selection through go-live and post-launch stabilization.

Program Management

Multi-workstream project tracking, dependency mapping, vendor coordination, milestone reporting, risk management, and executive dashboards. When you have 20+ teams and a dozen vendors all working toward the same go-live date, Coppermoon keeps it all moving.

About

Rod Garlick

Rod Garlick has spent 27 years helping financial institutions navigate technology transitions — from community credit unions to Fortune 500 banks. His work sits at the intersection of technology and operations: making sure that when an institution converts its core system or rolls out a new digital platform, everything works on day one.

Rod combines implementation delivery experience with a consultative sales background, which means he understands both what the technology can do and what the institution actually needs. Whether it's coordinating 24 teams across a multi-workstream core conversion or guiding a credit union through its first mobile banking launch, he brings the same hands-on project discipline.

Based in Utah. Available for contract engagements nationwide.
