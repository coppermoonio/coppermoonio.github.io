About Coppermoon

One advisor who's been in the room when it matters

Coppermoon Consulting was founded by Rod Garlick, a banking technology specialist with 27 years of hands-on experience managing implementations for financial institutions ranging from community credit unions to Fortune 500 banks.

Rod has worked across 50+ technology implementations spanning core system conversions, single system integrations, affiliate bank acquisition programs, digital banking platform rollouts, lending system migrations, and complex multi-vendor programs. He understands not just the technology, but the organizational dynamics, vendor politics, and regulatory landscape that determine whether a conversion succeeds or fails.

Coppermoon exists because community banks and credit unions deserve the same caliber of independent advisory that large institutions get from the big consulting firms — without the six-figure price tag, the team of junior consultants, or the three-month engagement timeline.

Every assessment is conducted personally by Rod, enhanced by AI-powered analytical tools for faster, more comprehensive analysis. Your institution gets direct access to decades of pattern recognition — not a methodology binder.
