Assessment Services

Four ways to reduce your risk

Each assessment is built on a comprehensive framework covering technology, data, people, vendors, compliance, costs, and governance. Modular by design — scoped to your situation.

Full Core System Conversion Readiness Assessment

The comprehensive assessment for institutions planning a full core platform migration. Covers every dimension — technology ecosystem, data, vendors, contracts, people, governance, regulatory, costs, and risk. Designed to answer one question: are you actually ready?

  • Complete technology ecosystem mapping
  • Data readiness & locus-of-data analysis
  • Vendor & contract evaluation with deconversion review
  • Department mapping — roles, duties, dependencies
  • Staff change management & resistance assessment
  • Data security & governance review
  • Regulatory disclosure requirements
  • Realistic cost & timeline analysis
  • Customer communication & attrition risk
  • Contingency planning
  • Risk scorecard with go/no-go recommendation

Limited Conversion or Single Product Implementation Assessment

For institutions planning a single system migration — lending platform, digital banking, card processing, or similar. Narrower scope, but the same rigorous framework applied to your specific project. Also ideal for institutions early in the evaluation process who want a focused readiness check before committing.

  • System-specific technology assessment
  • Integration point & dependency mapping
  • Data readiness for targeted migration
  • Vendor & contract review
  • Staffing & organizational readiness
  • Risk scorecard with recommendations

M&A Technology Due Diligence

For institutions considering an acquisition. Understand what you're buying before the deal closes — technology stack, integration complexity, contract liabilities, staff retention risks, and realistic timelines. Your findings can influence the deal price or the decision to proceed.

  • Target institution technology assessment
  • Integration cost & complexity analysis
  • Contract & licensing liability review
  • Staff retention & cultural factors
  • Customer attrition risk modeling
  • Deal impact recommendation

AI Implementation Readiness

For institutions evaluating AI tools — chatbots, fraud detection, document processing, lending automation. Cut through vendor hype and assess whether your data, infrastructure, and team are actually ready. Includes regulatory implications specific to AI in banking.

  • AI-specific data readiness assessment
  • Infrastructure & integration evaluation
  • Vendor claims reality check
  • Regulatory & compliance implications
  • Staff readiness & change management
  • ROI validation & timeline assessment

About Coppermoon

We deliver the assessment. You make the decision.

Coppermoon is led by Rod Garlick, who brings 27 years and 50+ implementations across community banks and credit unions. Every assessment is conducted personally by Rod — not handed off to junior staff.

We have hands-on experience managing implementations for financial institutions ranging from community credit unions to Fortune 500 banks. Our background spans both sides of the table — working for core processors, mortgage and consumer lending software companies helping with their conversions and implementations, as well as inside financial institutions evaluating technology deployment, vendor performance, and contract negotiations.

We have worked across technology implementations spanning core system conversions, single system integrations, affiliate bank acquisition and integration programs, digital banking platform rollouts, lending system migrations, and complex multi-vendor programs. Our experience includes evaluating community bank technology usage and making recommendations about which software should be kept or replaced during acquisitions, as well as financial institution compliance at the enterprise level.

We understand not just the technology, but the organizational dynamics, vendor politics, and regulatory landscape that determine whether a project succeeds or fails.

Coppermoon exists because community banks and credit unions deserve the same caliber of independent advisory that large institutions get from the big consulting firms — without the six-figure price tag, the team of junior consultants, or the multi-month engagement.

Every assessment is enhanced by AI-powered analytical tools for faster, more comprehensive analysis. Your institution gets direct access to decades of pattern recognition — not a methodology binder.
